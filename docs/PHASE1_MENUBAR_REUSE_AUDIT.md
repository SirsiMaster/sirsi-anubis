These are out of scope for step 1 and become later Lane B items:

SwiftUI's `MenuBarExtra` (macOS 13+) is the LEAN choice; need to confirm `LSMinimumSystemVersion=13.0` is acceptable (current bundle says 12.0).
3. **Existing iOS reuse.** `ios/Pantheon/` (App/Models/Services/Theme/Views) is step 3's audit. Naming the dirs here only â€” not the file-level audit.
