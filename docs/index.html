Anubis

Finds junk on your computer that cleaning apps miss.

hover to flip
$ pantheon weigh · $ pantheon judge

Scans 7 domains (caches, Docker, Homebrew, Python, Node, Go, AI models) with 58+ rules. Shows you exactly what's wasting space and safely deletes it.

Ka

Detects apps you deleted that are still secretly running.

hover to flip
$ pantheon ka

Finds "ghost apps" — uninstalled software that left behind startup files, system registrations, and cache folders. Scans Launch Services in parallel for sub-second results.

< 1sFull scan time
8.5s → 1sBefore/after

Hathor

Finds duplicate files eating your storage.

hover to flip
$ pantheon mirror

Three-phase dedup: groups by size → partial hash (first+last 4KB) → full SHA-256. Recommends which copy to keep. Includes a web UI for review.

3-phaseMatching algo
Web UIVisual review

Sekhmet

Kills runaway processes that freeze your machine.

hover to flip
$ pantheon guard

Audits RAM and CPU pressure. Classifies every process as system, user, zombie, orphan, or hog. Alerts when your IDE is about to stall and offers to intervene.

264%CPU caught
17 minFreeze prevented

Ma'at

Grades your code quality and enforces team standards.

hover to flip
$ pantheon maat · $ pantheon scales

Runs test coverage, lint checks, and commit traceability. Produces a "Feather Weight" score out of 100. Scales enforces org-wide policies as YAML rules.

Horus

Fixes broken search results and indexes your entire filesystem.
